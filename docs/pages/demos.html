A Better Cookie Message

Don't you find it irritating when the EU Cookie message display over the top of what you're trying to view, or worse, trying to click.
or when you are about to click and the message slides in, all fancy, and moves what you were trying to click.
My goal here will be to have a better message. One that doesn't obscure and has Fancy Sliding but doesn't get in the way.
